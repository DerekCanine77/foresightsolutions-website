Privacy

Privacy notice for Foresight Solutions LLC.

This page explains how Foresight Solutions may handle information submitted through email, contact forms, future scheduling tools, future analytics, or direct business conversations.

Information you provide

Foresight Solutions may receive contact details, company information, role information, project context, AI scale diagnostic intake details, and scheduling information when you choose to contact the firm or request a conversation.

How information may be used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, prepare for conversations, coordinate scheduling, evaluate fit, improve the intake process, and maintain business records related to prospective or active engagements.

Future analytics and tools

The site may later use Google Analytics, Google Search Console, LinkedIn Insight Tag, contact form services, or scheduling embeds. If those tools are added, they may collect technical, interaction, referral, conversion, or scheduling data according to their own policies and configuration.

Form submissions

When a live form endpoint is added, submitted form data may be processed by the selected form service before being delivered to Foresight Solutions. Do not submit confidential, regulated, or sensitive business information through the form unless an appropriate agreement is in place.

Scheduling data

If a scheduling link or embed is added, scheduling details may be processed by the selected scheduling provider, including name, email, meeting time, and notes provided during booking.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Derek.Canine@foresightsolutions.io.
