AIP readiness

AIP readiness means knowing where AI can safely influence enterprise work.

Foresight helps leaders evaluate whether data, business context, controls, workflows, and ownership are mature enough for AIP use cases to move from impressive demos to governed operational impact.

Use case fit

Prioritize AIP opportunities where decision support, exception handling, or operational acceleration is tied to a real business workflow.

Trusted grounding

Ensure AI interactions reference trusted business objects, relationships, policies, and actions rather than loose data fragments.

Governed action paths

Define where AI can inform, recommend, draft, route, or trigger action with appropriate human and system controls.

Data readiness

Assess lineage, freshness, completeness, and operational fitness for the domains AIP will touch.

Adoption design

Shape AI-enabled workflows around how operators, analysts, executives, and frontline teams actually work.

Risk and accountability

Clarify ownership, review paths, escalation rules, and release practices before AI becomes part of the operating cadence.

Pressure-test the path from AI capability to governed operational use.
