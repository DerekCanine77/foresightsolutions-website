Why now

Successful pilots create a new problem: the enterprise now has to make the work repeatable.

Early wins prove that AI can matter. They also expose the harder constraint: decisions still depend on informal knowledge, applications do not always change operating routines, and each new use case can feel like a bespoke effort. The next maturity step is building the repeatable environment around the technology.
