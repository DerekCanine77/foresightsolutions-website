Operating gaps

The pressure points that surface once Foundry moves from innovation story to operating expectation.

These are the gaps executive teams feel when the platform is working, but the enterprise is still not extracting the level of leverage it expected.

Ontology drift

Teams begin to model the same business in slightly different ways. Reuse falls. Governance gets harder. Scaling new applications becomes slower and less elegant.

Pipeline-to-workflow disconnect

Data is available and trustworthy, yet not sufficiently bound to the approvals, decisions, and writeback mechanisms that move operations.

Application adoption drag

Applications exist, but the surrounding work model does not fully shift. The business still relies on old channels to coordinate important decisions.

AIP without operational consequence

AI outputs are informative but not yet embedded inside governed business processes where they can safely influence action.
