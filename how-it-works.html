Model

A more useful way to isolate why Foundry is not compounding as fast as the business expected.

The aim is not to add more activity. It is to identify which layer of the operating model is constraining leverage.

Start with the business symptom

Expansion is slow. Adoption is narrow. AIP is still peripheral. Teams are not reusing enough. The first signal is usually operational, not technical.

Trace it to the real layer of drag

Ontology, data foundations, application design, workflow logic, or execution concentration usually sits underneath the visible symptom.

Focus on the highest-leverage constraint

The objective is to strengthen the part of the system that unlocks reuse, trust, adoption, and speed simultaneously.

Help the environment compound

The final test is simple: does the next use case become easier, cleaner, and more operationally meaningful than the last one?
